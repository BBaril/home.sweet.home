Lesley Fretz

Home Sweet Home was founded by Lesley Fretz in 2002. She is a dedicated worker and companion. Where some say ‘no’ she says, ‘no problem’. From musical therapy to meal preparation to basic home maintenance, she can help with a wide variety of tasks designed to help keep your loved ones safe, cared for and at home. She has experience with special needs children and adults, including end of life care, spanning decades with may references.
